Kathmandu

Kathmandu Valley

Kathmandu Valley mixes heritage, spiritual sites, and vibrant city life. It features Swayambhunath, Boudhanath, and Durbar Square as top attractions.

  • Ancient temples and palace courtyards
  • Hilltop views and sunrise panoramas
  • Cultural markets, museums, and local cuisine
Best for: culture & heritage Popular stops: 3
Lumbini

Lumbini

Lumbini is the spiritual heart of Nepal, shaped by Buddhist heritage, sacred gardens, and peaceful meditation areas that guide visitors through the birthplace of Lord Buddha.

  • Maya Devi Temple and sacred garden
  • Ashokan Pillar and ancient pathways
  • World Peace Pagoda and quiet reflection spots
Best for: spirituality & culture Popular stops: 4
Ilam

Ilam

Ilam offers cool mountain air, rolling tea hills, and scenic viewpoints. It is one of Nepal’s most peaceful retreats for nature lovers and slow travel.

  • Tea estate walks and green hill trails
  • Ridge viewpoints and photo routes
  • Local villages and fresh tea experiences
Best for: nature & slow travel Popular stops: 5
Badimalika

Badimalika

Badimalika in Sudurpashchim Nepal is a growing destination for clear mountain views, heritage stops, and quiet scenic exploration in western Nepal.

  • Scenic ridge walks and viewpoint trails
  • Local cultural encounters
  • Sunrise and sunset photography
Best for: heritage & scenic drives Popular stops: 4
